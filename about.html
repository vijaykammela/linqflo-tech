About LINQFLO

We connect the parts, pumps, and platforms that keep industrial fluid systems running — sourced, engineered, and delivered end-to-end.

What we do

LINQFLO is a solutions and marketplace layer for industrial, cooling, and process fluid systems. We work with manufacturers, EPC contractors, and plant engineering teams to source pumps, thermal management equipment, piping, fittings, valves, and monitoring hardware — then help specify, procure, and support it through the life of the system.
